About Me

Greetings!

Hi there! I'm Jihan Sharira Syahli, an Undergraduate Library and Information Science student at University of Indonesia. I was born and raised in the bustling city of Jakarta, where my love affair with literature found its roots. Books have been my interest as well as my sanctuary, opening doors to worlds unknown and feeding my insatiable curiosity. Beyond the realms of literature, I've found myself drawn to the intricate beauty of languages. Learning new languages isn't merely a hobby; it's an exploration of cultures and an avenue to connect with diverse communities. Yet, amidst these passions, one figure stands as my role model - my mother. She embodies the essence of a career-driven visionary while exuding boundless affection as a mother. Her ability to gracefully navigate the complexities of a successful career while nurturing our family has left an incredible mark on my aspirations. My motivation in life is that within every obstacle lies an opportunity for growth and there must be always something good behind everything. In every chapter of life, I believe that positivity can be found even in the most unexpected places.

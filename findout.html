Rebecca Sugar

Author of Steven Universe and many more.

Rebecca Rea Sugar (July 9, 1987) is an American animator, screenwriter and musician. She is best known for being the creator of the Cartoon Network series Steven Universe. Until 2013, Sugar was a writer and storyboard artist on the animated series Adventure Time. Her work on the two series has earned her seven Primetime Emmy Award nominations.
